    • Refund & Return Policy


    At Jubiliant Insurance Broking Service Pvt Ltd., insurance policies once issued and confirmed are non-returnable and non-cancellable. Due to the nature of insurance services, all sales are treated as final.

    However, a refund will be considered strictly in the limited case of duplicate (double) payment made for the same insurance policy number.

    1. Duplicate Payment Refund Eligibility

    • The customer has made more than one payment for the same policy.
    • The policy has been successfully issued only once.
    • The request is supported by valid proof of transaction.

    Only the excess / duplicate payment amount will be refunded.

    2. Non-Refundable Situations

    • Change of mind after purchase
    • Incorrect plan chosen by the customer
    • Cancellation request after policy issuance
    • Non-usage of the policy
    • Delay in raising the request
    • Any reason other than verified duplicate payment
    • If a claim has already been raised or processed

    3. Refund Process

    In case of a verified duplicate payment:

    • The request will be reviewed by our team.
    • Upon approval, the duplicate amount will be refunded to the original mode of payment.
    • The refund will be processed within 7–10 business days, depending on bank/payment gateway timelines.
    • A confirmation will be sent via Email / SMS / WhatsApp.

    4. How to Raise a Refund Request

    Customers can raise a refund request by contacting us:

    Email: care@jubiliant.co.in

    Phone / WhatsApp: +91 44 4204 8484 , +91 89259 44072

    Please provide the following details:

    • Policy Number
    • Transaction ID(s)
    • Payment Date
    • Proof of payment (screenshot or statement copy)

    This policy applies to all policies purchased through our website, mobile application, authorised agents, or payment links. By completing a purchase, the customer agrees to this Refund & Return Policy.

    Jubiliant Insurance Broking Service Pvt Ltd. reserves the right to modify this policy at any time without prior notice.
