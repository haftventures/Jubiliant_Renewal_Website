    We do not offer any products or services outside India. Your personal data will be primarily stored and processed in India. By accessing or using this Platform and its services, you expressly consent to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy, our Terms of Use, and applicable Indian laws including data protection laws. If you do not agree, please do not use our Platform.


    1. Collection of Information

    We collect personal and sensitive personal information when you:

    • Visit or use our Platform
    • Register or create an account
    • Purchase or renew an insurance policy
    • Submit an insurance proposal
    • Contact customer support

    Information collected may include:

    • Name, Date of Birth, Address
    • Mobile Number & Email ID
    • PAN / Aadhaar / Identity proof (if legally required)
    • Policy details
    • Bank account / Payment details (for premium or refund)

    Sensitive information such as biometrics or payment authentication may be collected only with your consent and as per law. Non-personal data such as IP address, browser type and device information may also be collected.

    If a third-party service collects your information, their privacy policy applies. We are not responsible for their practices.

    Important Advisory: Do not share your PIN, password, card details or OTP with anyone claiming to represent us. Report such incidents immediately.


    2. Use of Information

    We use your information for:

    • Insurance policy issuance and renewal
    • Premium payment processing
    • Claim processing & support
    • Identity verification & fraud prevention
    • Sending updates, reminders & notifications

    You may opt out of marketing communications anytime.
