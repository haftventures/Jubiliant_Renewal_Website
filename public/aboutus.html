    • About Us


    We, Jubiliant Insurance Broking Services Pvt Ltd, are an Insurance Broking and Consulting firm established in the year 2015. Our steady and successful journey is largely due to our excellent relationship with industry fraternity on one end, and satisfied clientele on the other.

    In the nascent phase, our focus has been on understanding customer expectations and fine-tuning our operations to cater to the dynamic demands of the industry. Be it individuals, small firms or big corporate houses, our clients are a delighted lot.


    Our Values

    Integrity

    We conduct our business with integrity. We establish the highest level of business ethics in our dealings with our customers and partners.

    Innovation

    We inspire innovation in all facets of our work. Our success is a result of exploring new approaches and concepts beyond conventional methods.

    Passion

    We are passionate about who we are and what we do. We strongly believe that people who are focused, self-motivated and inspired by what they do, transform their passion to others.

    Customer First

    We prioritise our customers above everything else. Their needs guide our decisions, processes, and service delivery to ensure complete satisfaction.


    Insurance at Your Convenience

    Gone are the days when people were physically visiting insurance companies for renewal, premium payment and other service requirements. Agents were an integral part of the insurance ecosystem and were acting as trusted intermediaries. But times have changed and now it is the brokers who act on behalf of their clients (the actual customers who buy insurance).


    Our Milestones

    Our awareness and adept mindset led to our evolution and development of new capabilities enabling us to thrive in the ever-changing insurance landscape. In a period when people were looking for convenient insurance buying options, we were the first to introduce Insurance Kiosks.

    We quickly expanded this prototype in organised retail stores across Tamil Nadu for ease of access to individual customers. We are now one of the pioneers in this insurance business model with our footprints across the country.


    Our Uniqueness

    Corporates usually spend time and efforts in the core production and execution activities such as sales & marketing, supply chain, logistics, finance, human resource management etc. There usually isn’t expertise and focus with respect to risk assessment and insurance solutions. This is where we come in.

    We are a trustworthy resource guiding our corporates in deciding the best insurance solutions for them. We also provide them with the best-in-the-industry risk assessment and consulting services, support them with policy placement solutions and also offer TAT-specified claim processing. We also proactively offer other insurance- related consulting & solutions.

    Today, we are associated with multiple corporates and are proud to be an integral part of their journey to success. Industry reference plays a vital factor in the growth of our corporate clientele base.
